Hello — I’m NYIRAGASIGWA Veronise. I study law at the University of Kigali and I build simple, useful websites and designs in my free time. I made this portfolio to show the work I care about and to keep learning by doing.

Why I made this portfolio

At first I wanted a place where people can see my projects and understand what I can do. My portfolio is not only for employers — it is a record of my learning. When I finish a project, I add it here so I can remember how I solved the problem and get better next time.

What I hope it shows

I try to make things that are clear and helpful. That means clean layouts, easy reading, and small interactions that feel natural. I also care about projects that help the community — for example my Eco Artistry Creations and the Green Fertilizer project.

"You learn by doing. Keep building, one small thing at a time."

A small message to you

If you are learning design or code, start with a simple project you care about. You don’t need fancy tools. Open a blank HTML file, make a small page, and keep improving it. Share your work — people will see progress more than perfection.

Don’t wait until everything is perfect. I started with small pages and fixed them many times. That was how I learned to design layouts and write CSS. Your first project will not be the best — but it will teach you something valuable.

How to use this portfolio

If you are curious about my work, check the Projects section for live examples. If you want to talk, use the Contact form — I respond to real messages and I enjoy helping students find their first steps.

Thank you for reading. If this inspired you, try building a small page today — then add it to a public folder and show it to one person. Small progress every day adds up.

— Veronise
